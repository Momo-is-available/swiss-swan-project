  • Why do identical products have varying prices? Is this practice allowed?
  • TheSwissSwan operates as an online marketplace that allows independent sellers to list and sell their products directly to buyers. Pricing is entirely determined by the sellers, with no interference from TheSwissSwan. This can lead to situations where the same product is listed at varying prices by different sellers. As a marketplace platform, TheSwissSwan fully adheres to all legal requirements related to hosting multiple sellers on its platform.
  • I noticed a product listed for Rs. 1000, but after clicking on it, multiple prices appear, and the size I want is priced at Rs. 1600. Why does the price differ on the product details page?
  • TheSwissSwan is an online marketplace where multiple sellers may offer the same style at different prices set individually by each seller. The price displayed on the listing page might not always reflect the lowest price for that style. This is because the seller showcased on the listing page is selected based on various factors, with price being just one of them. However, when you navigate to the product display page for a specific style, you can view all the prices offered by different sellers for that item on the platform.
  • How can I identify fraudulent emails or calls that attempt to steal sensitive personal and confidential information?

  • If you receive an email or a call from someone claiming to be from TheSwissSwan and requesting sensitive information like debit/credit card PINs, net banking, or mobile banking passwords, it is crucial that you never provide such personal or confidential details. TheSwissSwan or our affiliate logistics partners will never ask for this type of sensitive information. If you have already disclosed such information, please report it immediately to the relevant law enforcement authorities.

      Here are some common tactics fraudsters use to deceive consumers:
    • "Congratulations! You've been nominated as a 'Top TheSwissSwan customer' and are eligible for a luxury gift. Please share your proof of address and debit/credit card details to claim your prize."
    • "Hi, I'm calling from TheSwissSwan. We're pleased to inform you that you've won an exclusive lucky draw coupon worth Rs. 5000 on your recent purchase. Kindly share your credit/debit card number so we can transfer the amount directly into your account."

  • Refund of platform fee
    • If the order is lost or undelivered to the preferred location, the complete order amount will be refunded if paid online, including the platform fee.

    • Shipping fee is charged towards providing delivery and post-order services, including but not limited to, higher-than-average returns.

      • Orders below a certain value, which may vary based on the category:
        • Orders below INR 1199 for all categories except personal care and personal care orders below INR 299 (not applicable for Insiders) are charged with a shipping fee.

        • The threshold values can change, so please review the Shipping Fee at the time of checkout.

  • Refund of shipping fee
    • If the entire order is lost or undelivered to the preferred location, the complete order amount will be refunded if paid online, including the shipping fee.
    • If an order is cancelled partially, shipping charges will not be refunded. In case of whole order cancellation, shipping charges will be refunded.
    • In case an item/order is returned, the shipping fee is not refunded.
    • For accounts whose return behavior does not adhere to our fair usage policy, the shipping fee will be non-refundable irrespective of the order value*.
    • *Order value is calculated after applying discounts/VAT/GST or any other applicable charges.

TheSwissSwan reserves the right to levy a shipping fee on customers whose return rates are higher than average and who do not adhere to the liberal return service.

Returns and Exchange

You can return/exchange your orders in TheSwissSwan

  • What is TheSwissSwan's Return and Exchange Policy? How does it work?

  • TheSwissSwan provides an option for returning or exchanging products purchased from TheSwissSwan for any reason within the specified return/exchange period (refer to the product details page for the period). Returns will be picked up from the address where the product was delivered. Customers can try on the product, but it must remain unused and in its original condition with the tags and packaging intact. If the product comes with a Seal Tag, it should remain attached to avoid complications during returns or exchanges. The return process includes two options:

    • Pick-up: Available in most locations, this service is free. You’ll see the pick-up option when initiating a return request.
    • Self-Ship: If pick-up isn’t available in your area, we allow self-shipping. Shipping costs will be credited as TheSwissSwan credits, provided the product meets return policy requirements and you submit a scanned copy of the courier receipt.

    The delivery agent may perform a quality check during pick-up. If the pick-up is successful, a doorstep refund will be initiated for the return amount. If the return does not pass the quality check, it will be shipped back to you. In case of an exchange due to size mismatch or defective item, a free replacement will be provided, subject to stock availability and your address’s serviceability.

    Exchanges can be made for the same or different sizes of the same style, or for another item of equal or different value from TheSwissSwan within the specified exchange period, provided your address is serviceable.

    • The item for exchange must be returned in its original condition, with tags and packaging intact.
    • For an exchange, the replacement item will be delivered to the same address as the original item. If you want the exchange sent to a different address, it's recommended to return the item for a refund and place a new order.

    Exceptions and rules apply to certain items:

    • Items like Swarovski, precious jewelry, Rayban sunglasses, socks, briefs, shapewear bottoms, lingerie sets (with briefs), swimwear, mittens, wristbands, and certain other categories cannot be returned or exchanged.
    • Products like fine jewelry, watches, and specific items susceptible to damage have limited return/exchange periods. Products such as sherwanis can only be exchanged, not returned.
    • Intimate products like innerwear, sleepwear, and lingerie items can only be self-shipped for returns and cannot be exchanged.
    • All returned or exchanged products must be unused and in original condition with tags and packaging intact (e.g., shoes must be returned in the original shoe box).

    Exchange policy details:

    • If the exchanged item is of a higher value, the price difference will be charged. If the exchanged item is of lower value, the price difference will be refunded after the original item is picked up.
    • The net amount paid for the original item, excluding instant cashback/discounts, will be used to calculate the price difference for exchanges.
    • Refunds for exchanges will be initiated after the successful pick-up of the original item.
    • All exchanges are subject to stock availability and serviceability of your address.
    • Only one item can be exchanged at a time, although multiple items can be exchanged by initiating separate exchange requests.
    • Non-returnable products cannot be exchanged.
    • The platform handling fee will not apply to exchange orders.
    • TheSwissSwan reserves the right to restrict exchanges if the policy is misused, as determined by TheSwissSwan.
    • In case of free gifts/offer items associated with a product, if the main product is returned, the free gift must be returned as well.
    • TheSwissSwan will not be responsible for products returned by mistake. If an extra or incorrect item is returned, TheSwissSwan is not accountable for its replacement or delivery.

    Self-ship returns:

    • If you self-ship the return, ensure that the item is securely packed to prevent damage during transit. We recommend using a reliable courier service.
    • For self-shipped returns, shipping costs will be reimbursed if the return meets the policy and the courier receipt is validated. Refunds will only be initiated after the product passes a quality check at the warehouse. If the product fails the check, it will be reshipped back to you.

Card tokenization

  • What are the latest RBI guidelines?

  • The RBI has mandated that e-commerce platforms like TheSwissSwan and other payment gateways and aggregators cannot hold a shopper’s card details beyond September 30th, 2022. The official circular on tokenisation is available on the RBI website.

  • How do these guidelines affect me as a TheSwissSwan customer?

  • This means that with effect from September 30th, 2022, your original card details will be replaced with a code known as a ‘token’ in the system. You can choose whether you want to tokenize your card or not.

  • Does this mean I will have to enter my card details every time I make a transaction on TheSwissSwan?

  • No, the RBI has permitted us to securely tokenize card details after receiving approval. TheSwissSwan can tokenize the card with your permission and you can then use the secured card for every subsequent purchase without having to enter details each time.

  • What is the process of tokenization?

  • Tokenization refers to the replacement of actual card details with a code known as a ‘token’ on a merchant platform. This ensures that your card details are secured as TheSwissSwan will not save your card details.

  • What advantages does tokenization offer?

  • Since card details are not shared during transaction processing, tokenized card transactions are considered safer. A tokenized card also removes the need to enter details each time.

  • What will happen if I choose not to tokenize my card?

  • If you don't tokenize your card, you'll need to enter card details such as the card number, expiration date, and CVV for every payment you make on TheSwissSwan using that card.

  • Do I need to tokenize each of my cards individually?

  • Yes, your consent is only for one unique card. Therefore, all your cards must be tokenized separately. There is also no limit to the number of cards you can tokenize in your name.

  • If I have already tokenized my card on another website/platform, do I need to tokenize it again on TheSwissSwan?

  • Yes, your consent applies to one specific card on a particular platform or website. Therefore, your cards need to be tokenized on The Swiss Swan separately, even if they have already been tokenized on another platform.

  • What is meant by de-tokenization?
  • De-tokenization is the process of converting a token back into the original card details.
