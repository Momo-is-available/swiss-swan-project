Returns & Exchanges

Returns and exchanges are processed in accordance with the updated policy provided on the Product Description Page (PDP). Requests for return or exchange will be accommodated as per this policy.

For non-serviceable PIN codes, you may self-ship the product to our return desk. Please note that pickup and refund timelines may vary based on the courier service and the selected payment mode.

  • What is SwissSwan's Return and Exchange policy?

  • SwissSwan provides a hassle-free return and exchange policy.

    The availability of return and exchange will be mentioned on each product’s description page. Generally, it ranges from 2 to 14 days, except for certain high-priced products that are non-returnable.

    The availability of exchange service depends on courier partner services and pin code.

  • What is Style Exchange?

  • SwissSwan provides a style exchange facility where you can exchange a product from your order with a different size, color, or a completely new and different product from SwissSwan.

    If the new product’s amount is higher than the product you wish to exchange, you can pay that extra amount via cash on delivery or using any of the digital payment modes.

    If the new product’s amount is lower than the product you wish to exchange, you will get a refund for the excess amount either to your bank account or your original payment mode.

  • What is Self-Ship & how do I do it?

  • For a few pin codes and due to certain courier partners' limitations, SwissSwan is unable to provide the product pickup option during returns.

    If your pin code comes under this limitation, you can return the product by self-shipping the item to our return desk.

  • What are my Pick-Up timelines?

  • We always try to get the pick-up done at the earliest. However, based on the pin codes and courier partner assignment, the pick-up timeline may vary from 3-7 days.

    You can track the pick-up timeline for your active orders here.

  • What are my Refund timelines?

  • SwissSwan initiates refunds once the product gets picked up and the quality check is done. For self-shipped items, the return is initiated after it arrives at our return desk and passes the quality check.

    Refund time depends on the mode of refund selected by you.

    • For Bank Account: 1-3 business days after the item reaches us.
    • For Online Refund: 7-10 business days after the item reaches us.
    • For Wallets: 1 business day after the item reaches us.

    You can track the refund timeline for your active orders here.

  • What is Instant Cashback?

  • Instant Cashback is a type of offer that is directly applied to the product or order value in your cart. The cashback amount is credited to your bank account or credit card based on the respective bank's terms and conditions.

  • How does Instant Cashback recovery happen?

  • If you place an order using an Instant Cashback offer but partially cancel or return items, causing the order value to drop below the minimum threshold required by the offer terms, the cashback amount will be subject to recovery.

    The cashback will either be deducted from the refund amount or the order value will be adjusted accordingly.

    Cancellation and charges

    You can track the status of your order in the "My Orders" section. Cancellation and address modification are allowed depending on the current status of the order. For Cash on Delivery (COD) orders, online payment can be made if the option is available.

  • Extra charges, Convenience fee

  • A Convenience Fee of Rs. 79/- along with a Platform Fee of Rs. 20/- will apply to orders below Rs. 1199/-. For orders containing only personal care items priced under Rs. 299/-, a Convenience Fee of Rs. 19/- and a Platform Fee of Rs. 20/- will be applicable.

  • Cancellation Policy

  • You can now cancel an order if it is in the packed/shipped status, provided the cancel option is available on the App/Website/M-site.

    This applies to items purchased during sales as well.

    The amount paid will be refunded to the same payment method used during purchase, or you can choose to receive the refund as SwissSwan credits.

  • Refund timelines for Cancellation

  • Once your order is canceled, the refund process will be initiated.

    The refund time varies depending on the chosen refund method, as follows:

    • Bank Account: 1-3 business days after the item is received by us.
    • Online Refund: 7-10 business days after the item is received by us.
    • Wallets: 1 business day after the item is received by us.
